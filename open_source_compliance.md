### 3. 🤝 How to Reuse & Contribute
We believe in community collaboration for climate action. Here is how you can help:

*   **For Developers**:
    1.  **Fork** the repository on GitHub.
    2.  **Clone** it to your machine (`git clone ...`).
    3.  **Create a Branch** for your feature (e.g., `git checkout -b add-new-marine-data`).
    4.  **Push** your changes and submit a **Pull Request (PR)**.
*   **For Researchers**:
    *   You can download our CSV datasets inside `backend/` and use them for your own analysis.
    *   If you have better data, you can open an "Issue" on GitHub to share it with us!
